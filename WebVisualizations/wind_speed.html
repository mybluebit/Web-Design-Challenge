Wind Speed

As expected, there is little to no relationship between latitude and wind speed. But we do notice that many of the cities in the northern hemisphere have strong wind speeds than there are in the cities in the southern hemisphere. Wind speed tends to stay between 0 and 12mph whether the cities are located in northern or southern hemisphere.
